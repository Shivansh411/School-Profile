About Our School

Global Public School is a premier educational institution committed to providing a holistic learning experience that nurtures intellectual curiosity, creativity, and character development in every student.

Our History

Founded in 1998, GPS has grown from a small neighborhood school to one of the most respected educational institutions in the region, serving over 1,200 students across all grade levels.

Our Mission

To empower students to achieve their full potential by providing a dynamic learning environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility.
